Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q1: What types of orthopedic treatments do you provide?

A: We provide joint replacement, fracture fixation, arthroscopic surgery, spinal surgery, sports injury treatment, and physiotherapy.

Q2: How do I know if I need surgery or non-surgical treatment?

A: The doctor will evaluate your condition with a physical exam and tests like X-rays or MRI before recommending the best treatment.

Q3: How long is the recovery time after surgery?

A: Recovery depends on the type of surgery. For example, joint replacement may take a few weeks to months, while arthroscopic surgery usually has a faster recovery.

Q7: What insurance plans do you accept?

Q8: How do I prepare for orthopedic surgery?

A: The doctor will give you specific instructions before surgery, like fasting, medication adjustments, and necessary tests.

Q9: Are there any risks or complications from surgery?

A: Like any surgery, there are risks such as infection or blood clots, but we take all precautions to minimize them.

Q10: Do you provide physiotherapy after surgery?

A: Yes, we offer physiotherapy and rehabilitation to help you recover faster and regain strength.

Q11: Can I get a consultation online or by phone?

A: Currently, consultations are in-person, but you can call us for any questions or preliminary advice.

Q12: What should I do in case of a sports injury?

A: Rest, ice the area, and contact our clinic as soon as possible for evaluation and treatment.

Q13: How soon can I return to work after surgery?

A: It depends on your surgery type and job. The doctor will advise you during follow-up visits.

Q14: Do you treat both adults and children?

A: Yes, we treat patients of all ages with orthopedic conditions.

Q15: What payment methods do you accept?

A: We accept cash, card payments, and insurance-based cashless treatments.
