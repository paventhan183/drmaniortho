Orthopedic & Specialized Care

  • Knee pain, ligament injuries, and arthritis

    We diagnose precisely and start with physio/medicines. Surgery only if needed.

  • Back or neck pain, slipped disc, and sciatica

    Posture care, spine physio, medicines; advanced options for severe nerve compression.

  • Shoulder pain, stiffness, and tendon tears

    Frozen shoulder care, injections, and arthroscopic (keyhole) repair when required.

  • Hip pain, fractures, and arthritis

    Hip preservation and fracture fixation; hip replacement for advanced arthritis.

  • Trauma & Fracture Management

    Emergency stabilization and modern fixation for safe, quick recovery.

  • Sports injuries

    Sports physio, ligament repair, and guided return-to-sport plans.

  • Joint Replacement Surgery

    Advanced implants and minimally invasive techniques for long-term comfort.

  • Spine Care & Surgery

    Balanced plan: conservative care first; surgery only when essential.

  • Clinical Laboratory Services

    In-house blood and health tests for faster, accurate decisions.

  • Physiotherapy

    Personalized rehab plans and modern equipment for quick recovery.

  • Digital X-Ray

    Clear imaging on the spot to speed up treatment.

  • Foot, ankle, hand, and wrist injuries or pain

    Early diagnosis, splints, physio, and minor procedures when needed.
